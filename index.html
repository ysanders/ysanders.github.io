I am a Canadian-Australian quantum physicist specialising in quantum computer science. I work on application-focussed quantum algorithms, analysis and mitigation of noise in quantum hardware, and various topics in fundamental quantum information science.

I was born Canadian but grew up mostly in Sydney, Australia. I currently live and work in Sydney, though I completed my post-secondary education in Canada.

I am currently employed by the University of Technology, Sydney. I prefer to be contacted by email and do not use social media very much.
